# G*BOY SPECIAL Style Guide

This document outlines the design system, component styles, and aesthetic choices of [gboyspecial.com](https://gboyspecial.com), allowing for a faithful recreation of the site's look and feel.

## 1. Design Philosophy & Aesthetic
- **Theme**: Retro-futuristic, Cyberpunk, Industrial.
- **Vibe**: High contrast, monospaced typography, raw and utilitarian but polished.
- **Key Visuals**: Dark backgrounds, bright neon accents (Yellow/Blue), blocky layout elements.

## 2. Typography
The site relies heavily on a single monospaced font family to achieve its distinct look.

- **Primary Font**: `Roboto Mono`, monospace.
- **Fallback**: `Courier New`, `monospace`.
- **Weights**:
  - **Regular**: Body text.
  - **Medium (500)**: Navigation links, secondary buttons.
  - **Bold (700)**: Primary Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons, Headings.
- **Text Sizes**:
  - **Standard/Body**: `text-xs` (approx 12px) or `text-sm` (approx 14px).
  - **Headings**: Scaled up, but often kept relatively understated to maintain the "terminal" feel.

## 3. Color Palette

### Backgrounds
- **Page Background**: `rgb(0, 0, 0)` (Black) - The main canvas is pitch black.
- **Card/Container Background**: `rgb(0, 0, 0)` (Black) - Often used with borders or specific aspect ratios to define shape.

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text**: `rgb(255, 255, 255)` (White) - Used for most content against the black background.
- **Secondary/Dark Text**: `rgb(0, 0, 0)` (Black) - Used on bright buttons or light backgrounds.
- **Accent Text**: `rgb(255, 255, 22)` (Yellow) or `rgb(60, 70, 255)` (Blue) - Used for hover states or emphasis.

### Brand Accents (Buttons & Highlights)
- **Neon Yellow**:
  - **Hex**: `#FFFF16`
  - **RGB**: `255, 255, 22`
  - **Usage**: Primary CTA ("GET BADGE"), Highlights.
  - **Hover**: `#EFEF06` (Slightly darker/muted yellow).
- **Electric Blue**:
  - **Hex**: `#3C46FF`
  - **RGB**: `60, 70, 255`
  - **Usage**: Secondary CTA ("MEMBERS TELEGRAM").
  - **Hover**: `#2C36EF` (Slightly darker blue).

## 4. UI Components

### Buttons
Buttons are defined by sharp corners (or slightly rounded), high contrast, and bold text.

**Primary CTA (Yellow)**
- **Background**: `#FFFF16`
- **Text**: Black (`#000000`)
- **Font Weight**: Bold
- **Padding**: `px-4 py-1.5` (approx 16px horizontal, 6px vertical)
- **Border Radius**: `rounded` (approx 4px)
- **Hover Effect**: Background darkens to `#EFEF06`.

**Secondary CTA (Blue)**
- **Background**: `#3C46FF`
- **Text**: White (`#255, 255, 255`)
- **Font Weight**: Medium
- **Padding**: `px-4 py-1.5`
- **Border Radius**: `rounded`
- **Hover Effect**: Background darkens to `#2C36EF`.

**Navigation Links**
- **Text**: White or Light Gray.
- **Hover**: Switches to Accent Color (Yellow or Blue) or simply brightens.
- **Style**: `text-xs`, `font-medium`, uppercase often used.

### Cards / Image Containers
- **Style**: Blocky, often with `overflow-hidden`.
- **Background**: Black.
- **Aspect Ratios**:
  - Small/Mobile: `aspect-[183/121]`
  - Medium/Desktop: `aspect-[435/276]`
  - Large: `aspect-[870/276]`
- **Behavior**: These containers often hold images or interactive elements that scale responsively.

### Navigation Bar
- **Layout**: Flexbox row (`flex items-center justify-between`).
- **Spacing**: `gap-2` (8px) between items.
- **Background**: Dark/Black (blends with page).
- **Sticky**: Often sticky at the top.

## 5. Layout & Spacing
- **Grid/Flex**: Extensive use of Flexbox for alignment (`items-center`, `justify-center` or `justify-between`).
- **Container Widths**: Content is typically constrained to a central column on larger screens but spans full width on mobile.
- **Spacing**:
  - Tight spacing is common (`gap-2`, `p-2`) to maintain a compact, dense information density typical of "system" interfaces.
  - Margins are used to separate major sections.

```css
    colors: {
      'gb-yellow': '#FFFF16',

      'gb-black': '#000000',
    },

      mono: ['"Roboto Mono"', 'monospace'],
    },
    aspectRatio: {
      'card-sm': '183 / 121',
      'card-md': '435 / 276',
    }
  }
}
```

## 7. Assets & Media
- **Images**: Often treated as "content blocks" fitting precisely into the aspect-ratio containers.
- **Icons**: Minimalist, often SVG, matching the text color.
